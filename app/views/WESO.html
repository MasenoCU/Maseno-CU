About WESO

Learn more about the be Western Outreach- Maseno University Christian Union hereby abbreviated as (WESO).

WESO is a non-political, non-denominational, non-profit making society affiliated to WESO-KENYA committed to preaching the gospel of salvation to All nations

Our History

Western Outreach (WESO) is an Evangelistic Team comprised of students mainly from western Kenya. It is responsible for organizing missions, in reach on campus, high school empowerment, and discipleship.

Mandate

To prepare and equip Christian students to take out the good news of salvation to the whole world and be effective people in the society. To develop and strengthen the spiritual life of members by prayers, brethren fellowships and bible study.

Vision

Every convert a disciple, every disciple a soul winner, and every pastor church leader equipped for the service in the church of the Lord Jesus Christ.

Mission

To grow up mature disciples, ready to preach the gospel of salvation to the lost and to train pastors’ church leaders for effective ministry.

Membership

Full membership shall be open to undergraduate student, member of the MUCU from the following regions; Bungoma, Busia,Kakamega,Vihiga Trans-nzoia. Any other interested member from other region who is willing to partner with weso and abide with weso constitution.There shall be no membership fee.

"See, hear and proclaim"

Activities

  • Fellowships every Saturday from 5:30pm-7:00pm
  • Prayer and fasting every Thursday
  • A retreat once per semester
  • At least one prayer week per semester, and another before every mission
  • Family meetings on Sundays
  • Door-to-door evangelism within the campus
  • Annual General Mission and Mini-missions
